LegitQuest’s Campus Ambassador Programme

It is the authority which makes law, to find it is arduous. A good lawyer is one who knows where to find it.

What you get?

  • Access to LegitQuest’s product
  • Certificate of appreciation
  • Networking with like-minded people in your fraternity
  • Opportunity to work in close collaboration with the Team LegitQuest to gain relevant experience
  • Exposure to the real life learning during the journey by being our proponent

What you do?

  • Be the official ambassador and representative of LegitQuest in your campus
  • Promote LegitQuest’s products on your campus and other approachable vicinity
  • Organise and coordinate various activities on your campus
  • Update us on the activities undertaken on a monthly basis

Eligibility

  • Law students who are in their third or fourth year of 5 year integrated LLB program
  • Law students who are in their second year of 3 year LLB program
  • Law students who have leadership qualities
  • Law students who have strong communication skills
